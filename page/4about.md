I am a 5th-year undergraduate student at Northeastern University majoring in computer science and mathematics. I  am  interested  in  developing  machine  learning  algorithms  and  applying  them  in the  context  of  biology  and  healthcare.   More  specifically,  I  am  interested  in  semi-supervised learning, learning with unlabeled and unbalanced data, graph-based learning, genomics, and clinical informatics. Currently I am a member of [Radivojac Statistical Methods's lab](https://www.ccs.neu.edu/home/radivojac/). In the past I have been a data scientist fellow at the [National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases](https://www.niaid.nih.gov/), and a software engineer at [Chewy](http://chewy.com) and [BlockTEST](https://blocktest.com/). Check out the Projects page and my CV for some of the works I have done!
